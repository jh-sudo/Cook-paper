McDonald’s McGriddles are back for a limited time

When it comes to seasonal McDonald’s specials, the McGriddles is indisputably S tier. It’s the one breakfast sandwich everyone gets hyped overseeing on the menu, even though they have been absent for a couple of months, as noticed by a few of our colleagues. Good news for McGriddles fans: McDonald’s has now brought back the popular sandwich! It’s available at all outlets, for breakfast only.

As for prices, expect to fork out $6.20 for Sausage McGriddles with Egg. The Chicken McGriddles with Egg will start at $6.20. There is also an upsized McGriddles Stack, served with egg, two sausage patties, cheese, and turkey bacon for $7. Head down to your nearest McDonald’s outlet for your McBreakfast fix soon, and don’t forget to scour their app for deals while you’re at it. We’re not sure how long the sandwich will stay on the menu, so fastest fingers first. Here’s hoping McDonald’s finally decides to make this a permanent menu item, the way they did for the Chicken McCrispy.
